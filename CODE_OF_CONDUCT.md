# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

StellarScout is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for all community members, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Welcome diverse perspectives
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Use inclusive language
- Be patient and understanding with others

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments or slurs
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct which reasonably could be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community members violating this code may be subject to:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from the community

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation:

1. Report to maintainers via: conduct@stellarscout.dev
2. Include details: who, what, when, where
3. Provide context and any evidence

All reports are confidential and investigated promptly.
